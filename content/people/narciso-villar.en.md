Researcher and academic with a robust multidisciplinary background integrating Industrial Engineering, Pure Mathematics, and Physics, consolidated with a Ph.D. in Biological Sciences. His primary research focuses on the application of biophysical and mathematical models to study tumor growth kinetics, as well as the antitumor mechanisms of electromagnetic fields and electrical bioimpedance in oncology patients. He has significantly contributed to scientific literature through the analysis of mathematical formulations, such as the Gompertz equation, and the development of simulations on electrical parameters in biological tissues. Furthermore, he combines his research endeavors with an extensive teaching career at both undergraduate and graduate levels in prestigious higher education institutions.

### Professional Experience

- Planning and Development Coordinator, Centro UASD La Vega (October 2024 – April 2025).
- Adjunct Professor, School of Physics, Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo (UASD) (2007 – Present).
- Professor, School of Sciences and Industrial Engineering, Universidad Católica del Cibao (UCATECI) (2001 – Present).
- Professor, Department of Mathematics, Instituto Superior de Formación Docente Salomé Ureña (ISFODOSU) (2021 – 2023).
- Professor, Department of Mathematics, Universidad Adventista Dominicana (UNAD) (2010).
- Mathematics Teacher (Secondary Level), Colegio Bilingüe Padre Fantino (2006).
