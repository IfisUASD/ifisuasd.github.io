Erika Alexandra Montero Lebron is a distinguished educator and researcher specializing in the field of Physics Education, complemented by a solid scientific background in Nanoscience. Her expertise focuses primarily on curriculum planning and development under the competency-based approach, leading crucial processes of redesigning and updating curricula in higher and technical-vocational education. Through her academic leadership, she drives pedagogical innovation in science teaching, coordinating institutional strengthening projects and directing strategies for educational assessment and quality.
