# About Me

I am the Principal Investigator at the UASD Institute of Physics, specializing in particle physics and complex systems. I have led several projects funded by FONDOCYT and MESCYT, and have published more than 30 articles in high-impact journals. My current focus is on the numerical simulation of neutrino interaction processes in the interstellar medium.

## Experience

- **UASD Institute of Physics** – Director of Research (2018 - present)
- **University of the Republic** – Associate Professor (2014 - 2018)
- **National University of Colombia** – Postdoctoral Researcher (2011 - 2013)

## Research Interests

- Particle physics
- Astroparticles
- Numerical simulation
- Data science applied to physics
