Doctor of Education specializing in Research and University Professor-Researcher affiliated with the Faculty of Sciences at the Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo (UASD). He serves as an Honorary Attached Researcher in the National Career of Researchers (CNI) of the Dominican Republic, focusing his scientific work in the areas of Physico-Chemical Sciences, Mathematics Education, and Science Education. His primary line of research delves into ICT-mediated educational innovation, with an emphasis on the impact of the flipped classroom model and digital competencies on the academic performance of physics and mathematics students. He possesses a robust educational background that includes a Master's and Specialization in Didactics of Mathematics, as well as a Specialization in Physics, supporting his scholarly output in indexed journals and international conferences.
