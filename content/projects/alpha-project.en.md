# Project Description

The **FONDOCYT-2024-01** project's main objective is to simulate the interaction of neutrinos in the Earth's atmosphere using advanced numerical methods and particle physics models. It aims to improve the accuracy of predictions of neutrino events detected by neutrino telescopes, contributing to the knowledge of high-energy physics and astrophysics.

## Methodology

- Development of Monte Carlo-based simulation algorithms for generating neutrino events.
- Modeling of interaction processes with the atmosphere, considering scattering and absorption effects.
- Validation of results by comparison with experimental data from observatories such as IceCube and ANTARES.
- Code optimization to run on GPU compute clusters.

## Impact

- Improved understanding of neutrino physics and their role in the evolution of the universe.
- Contribution to the particle physics community through the publication of results and the dissemination of simulation software.
- Strengthening of scientific research at the University of the Association of the States of the UASD.
