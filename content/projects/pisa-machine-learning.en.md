### Abstract

This research proposal seeks to explore the influence of teachers' and educational centers'
characteristics on the development of physics competencies in students, using PISA data. A quantitative
approach will be used, and statistical analysis and machine learning techniques will be implemented to
identify the variables that contribute most to student performance. Among the characteristics to be
analyzed are the teachers' training, the resources available at the educational center, and the
pedagogical strategies employed. The findings from this research have the potential to inform the
development of educational policies and the implementation of pedagogical practices that improve
student performance in physics and promote interest in STEM disciplines, in line with the objectives of
the Strategic Plan for Science, Technology, and Innovation.
