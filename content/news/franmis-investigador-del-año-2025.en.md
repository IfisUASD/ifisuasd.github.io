The Institute of Physics of the Faculty of Sciences at the Universidad Autónoma de Santo Domingo (UASD) is pleased to announce that researcher **Dr. Franmis J. Rodríguez Jiménez** has been awarded, for the second consecutive year, the title of **Researcher of the Year 2025** for the San Francisco de Macorís Campus.

Dr. Rodríguez Jiménez, a distinguished member of our institute, has been recognized for his significant contributions and scientific production in the field of **Physics Education**.

The award was presented by the Vice-Rector for Research and Postgraduate Studies, Dr. Radhamés Silverio González, during the **2025 Recognition Ceremony for Researchers, Coordinators, and Postgraduate Professors**, held at the university's Aula Magna.

The event was also attended by distinguished academic authorities, including the General Director of the San Francisco de Macorís Campus, **Prof. Roberto A. Marte G.**; the Academic Sub-director, **Prof. Judith Martínez**; the Dean of the Faculty of Sciences, **Prof. José Ferreira Capellán**; and the Director of the Institute of Physics, **Dr. Vladimir Pérez**.

From the Institute of Physics, we extend our most sincere congratulations to Dr. Rodríguez Jiménez for this well-deserved achievement, encouraging him to continue contributing to academic excellence and the scientific development of our institution.
