About Us

Vision, Mission, and Values of the Institute of Physics.

The Institute of Physics (IFIS) is established as an academic entity attached to the Faculty of Sciences of the Universidad Aut√≥noma de Santo Domingo (UASD). Its fundamental mission lies in the development of high-level scientific research in the field of Physics, as well as in related areas where significant interdisciplinary convergence exists.

Established on May 18, 1984, through Resolution No. 122-84 of the Honorable University Council, the IFIS represents the institutional evolution of the Dr. Gustavo Rathe Nuclear Sciences Laboratory, which was formerly part of the Department of Physics (now the School of Physics).

Currently, the IFIS distinguishes itself as one of the most prolific and active entities within the UASD. Backed by a highly qualified team of researchers and consistent scientific production in indexed journals, the Institute prioritizes both the generation of cutting-edge knowledge and the comprehensive training of the next generation of scientists.

Mission

To conduct basic and applied research in physics and related areas to promote the sustainable development of the country and its competitive positioning at both the national and international levels.

Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence and academic and scientific leadership nationally and internationally, distinguished by our high professionalism, the training of competent researchers, and the creation and contribution of knowledge, technologies, and innovative, high-quality products that benefit the country's sustainable development.

Values

The actions of the Institute of Physics will be conducted in accordance with the following ethical principles:

  • Respect and Inclusivity: We strive to create an inclusive and respectful work environment where all individuals feel valued and appreciated, regardless of their race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or any other individual or cultural difference.
  • Ethical Transparency and Integrity: We will be clear and honest in our communications and actions, always acting in accordance with the highest ethical and professional standards.
  • Loyalty and Confidentiality: We will maintain a relationship of trust and respect with the people we interact with, respecting their privacy and confidentiality at all times.
  • Responsibility and Equality: We assume responsibility for our actions and for making ethical and responsible decisions, treating all individuals with justice and impartiality, without discrimination.
  • Equity and Relevance: We focus on the problems and challenges that are most relevant and significant to our community, ensuring that everyone has access to the same opportunities and resources.
  • Objectivity and Justice: We will be impartial and base our decisions on facts and evidence, acting fairly and equitably without favoring some individuals over others.
  • Academic Excellence: We seek excellence in our research, teaching, extension, and service provision, maintaining high standards of quality and scientific rigor.
