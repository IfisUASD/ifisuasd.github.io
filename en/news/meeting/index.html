Annual Research Meeting of the Institute of Physics

28 Feb 2025

The scientific community of the UASD Institute of Physics met on February 28 in the central auditorium for the Annual Research Meeting. During the event, progress on ongoing projects was presented, preliminary results of neutrino simulation and supernova observation were shared, and new lines of research for the year 2026 were discussed.

Speakers included:

  • Dr. Vladimir Pérez – Director of Research, who summarized the findings of the FONDOCYT-2024-01 project.
  • Dr. Ana Sánchez – Co-investigator, with a panoramic view of supernova detection techniques applied in the MESCYT-2024-02 project.
  • Guest scientists from the National University of Colombia and the University of Chile, who presented international collaborations.

The meeting was also attended by undergraduate and graduate students, who shared their experiences in the laboratories and work with high-resolution telescopes. Emphasis was placed on the importance of interdisciplinarity and the training of local talent in areas of particle physics, astrophysics, and data science.

The Institute of Physics thanks all participants and announces that next year new projects will be launched and the collaboration network with national and international institutions will be expanded.
